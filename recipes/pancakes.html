Pancakes

Description

Soft and fluffy pancakes, perfect for breakfast or dessert.

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, eggs, and sugar into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a pan with butter.
  3. Pour batter into the pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Cook until golden.
