Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Home

Description

A simple Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chilli flakes. Quick and full of flavour.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Chilli flakes
  • Salt
  • Parsley (optional)

How to cook

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until cooked.
  2. Gently cook sliced garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add chilli flakes and remove from heat.
  4. Toss pasta with the garlic oil.
  5. Serve Immediately
